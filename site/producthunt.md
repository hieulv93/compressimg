# Product Hunt

## Tagline (60 chars max)
Compress images in your browser — files never leave your device

## Description (260 chars max)
Free image compressor for JPG, PNG, and WebP. Reduces file size up to 90% with no quality loss. 100% browser-based — your photos are never uploaded to any server. No sign-up, no watermarks, no limits.

## First Comment (Founder's comment — viết như đang nói chuyện thật)
Hi Product Hunt! 👋

I built CompressImg because I was frustrated with image compressor tools that upload your files to their servers. For personal photos or client work, that's a real privacy concern.

The entire compression pipeline runs in your browser using a Web Worker — so your files never leave your device, and the UI never freezes during compression.

A few things I'm proud of:
- LCP under 2 seconds on mobile (took a while to get there)
- Works with drag & drop, click, or Ctrl+V paste from clipboard
- Quality slider shows you the actual KB saved before you download

It's completely free — no account needed, no watermarks, no usage limits. I'm monetizing through non-intrusive ads that only appear after compression is done (not before, not during).

Would love to hear your feedback, especially on mobile UX. What would make this more useful for your workflow?

Link: https://compressimg.pro/compress-image
